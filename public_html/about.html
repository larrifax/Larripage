Kristian Tryggestad

I am a 23 year old developer from Molde (in Norway), currently living in Trondheim, Norway.

In the summer of 2007 I completed my three-year course at high school. Since then, I have studied computer science at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU).

In 2010 I completed my bachelor's degree in computer science, and immediately began studying for a master's degree with a specialization in systems engineering and human-machine interaction (HMI).

This course focuses on interdisciplinary studies of information science in practice: Information science and organizational development, design of user interfaces, multimedia and learning programs, systems development and quality assurance.

NTNU's description of the HMI study programme.

I have long been a major computer enthusiast who has eyes open for innovative solutions and products. I enjoy working in challenging environments where I can learn from and share experience with my peers, and in environments where there is a high focus on quality, good communication and software craftsmanship and where innovation using new technology and practices are in focus.
